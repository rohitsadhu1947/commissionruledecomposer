Three decisions that shape the journey

Q1 · When do we call the Quote API?

  • Option A — At loan approval stage (current working assumption): quotes generated once the loan is approved.
  • Option B — Earlier, during the agent's pitch: so the agent can disclose the insurance cost up front as part of the loan conversation.
  • Decides where Step 1 sits in the journey.
Confirmed

Q2 · What triggers policy issuance?

  • Issuance fires the moment loan disbursement is triggered — never before.
  • Insurer selection, Good Health Declaration and OTP are captured earlier and held by Ensuredit.
  • At disbursement, issuance proceeds instantly.
Open · out of scope for v1

Q3 · How are partial / multiple disbursements handled?

  • If a loan is disbursed in tranches (e.g. ₹10L approved, ₹2.5L first), do we issue one policy for the full sum, or one policy per tranche (child chain under the same Loan ID)?
  • Excluded from the v1 workflow below — to be confirmed before scoping.
Why it matters: Q1 affects regulatory cost disclosure; Q2 is locked and drives the hold-pattern design; Q3 determines whether the workflow needs a repeatable child-policy chain.

Process Overview

Four phases, end-to-end

  • 1 · Quote — At the Loan Approved stage*, the LMS/LOS sends the full dataset to Ensuredit. Ensuredit calls insurer APIs and returns collated quotes, which are surfaced in the agent app. *timing subject to Q1
  • 2 · Select & Verify — In the app the customer chooses an insurer and completes the Good Health Declaration and OTP verification; the LMS/LOS pushes this to Ensuredit.
  • 3 · Hold — Ensuredit marks the insurer, stores the Good Health data + OTP confirmation, and places the case in a hold pattern awaiting disbursement.
  • 4 · Issue & Deliver — When disbursement is triggered, the LMS/LOS calls for issuance; Ensuredit issues the policy and returns it. The app stores it to the customer account / Loan ID and notifies the customer via email + SMS.
Core principle: Policy issuance is triggered only on loan disbursement — selection & verification happen earlier and are held.
