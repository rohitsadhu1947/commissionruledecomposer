Q1 (Open) · When do we call the Quote API?

Option A: at the Loan Approved stage (current assumption). Option B: earlier, during the agent's pitch, so the insurance cost can be disclosed to the customer up front. Needs confirmation — sets where Step 1 sits.

Q2 (Confirmed) · What triggers policy issuance?

Issuance fires the moment loan disbursement is triggered — never before. Selection, Good Health Declaration and OTP are captured earlier and held.

Q3 (Open) · Partial / multiple disbursements?

If a loan disburses in tranches — one policy for the full sum, or one per tranche (child chain under the same Loan ID)? Excluded from this v1 workflow; to be confirmed.

Hold pattern: After insurer selection + Good Health Declaration + OTP verification (all in the SK Finance app), Ensuredit stores the data and holds the case as On Hold – Awaiting Disbursement. Issuance only proceeds when disbursement triggers it; the policy is then stored to the customer account / Loan ID and the customer is notified (email + SMS).
